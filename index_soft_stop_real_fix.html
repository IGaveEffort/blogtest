FAQ

Questions from brands & students, answered.

Brands

What kind of brands do you work with? +
We partner with consumer-facing brands that want to build a real relationship with students, from beverages and lifestyle to fintech, housing, and career platforms.
How do you price campaigns? +
Pricing depends on the number of campuses, creators, ambassadors, and activations involved. We offer one-time launch campaigns and semester-long programs.
Can you work with our in-house marketing team? +
Yes. We often act as the “campus arm” of existing teams, feeding student insight and UGC into your broader strategy.

Students

Do I need a huge following to join? +
No. We work with micro-creators, campus leaders, and students who are just well-connected on their campus, engagement and fit matter more than follower count. We suggest having at least 1,000 followers on TikTok or Instagram.
Is this remote or in-person? +
Many campaigns blend both: you may be posting from your own accounts while also helping with in-person activations on your campus.
Is it paid? +
Yes. Most roles include a mix of payment, free product, and performance-based bonuses. Details vary by brand and campaign.
